## Make a puzzle!
<Steps>
1. Use this [website](https://draradech.github.io/jigsaw/jigsaw.html) to make a jigsaw puzzle; you can keep all the settings the same except for the size. 
2. Download the SGV and import it into Adobe Illustrator on the laser cutting computers. 
3. From there fill in the jigsaw puzzle with any grayscaled picture you want! 
4. If the image is too big for the puzzle, you can use the masking tool in Adobe Illustrator to clip off the edges.
5. Cut and engrave as normal. Ask a PI to check over your work then your good to go!
</Steps>

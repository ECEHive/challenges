## Sublimate onto a keychain or t-shirt!
1. Find a design you would like to put on a shirt and print it out using the Sawgrass Printer in the Crafts Area. (Make sure to flip your design horizontally first! Remember that it turns out backwards when you sublimate)
2. Turn on the sublimation press and wait for it to reach the appropriate heat level.
3. Place your material on the bed, followed by your design, and some parchment paper.
4. Press down the upper half of the sublimation press and lock it for the appropriate time. Once it’s done, you can open it up and check out your new sublimated design!
5. Show your new keychain/shirt to a front desk PI to get a stamp!

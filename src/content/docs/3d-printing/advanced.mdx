## Design your own model using CAD software and print it out!
<Steps>
1. Get familiar with any CAD software (Fusion 360 - free for students, TinkerCad - free for all, SolidWorks - available at some Hive Benchtops, etc.)
2. Design a model of your choosing from scratch OR edit a pre-existing model with something like your name, initials, or any edit of your choice.
3. Queue up your model with a PI downstairs.
4. Pick up your model when it’s done and see how it turned out!
5. Show your own print to a front desk PI to get a stamp!
</Steps>

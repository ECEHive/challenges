## Print out a design of your choice using resin!
1. Google how resin printing works, and choose which type of resin you want for your print!
2. Browse sites like ([Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/)/[Printables](https://www.printables.com/)) to find a 3D model to print OR CAD your own.
3. Queue it up with a PI in the Semi-clean room downstairs.
4. Once your print is finished, you’ll receive an email from our automatic queue system. Pick up your print from the gray bins outside the room!
5. Show your new resin print to a front desk PI to get a stamp!

## Make the training fish
1. Make sure you have someone else in the room with you. 
2. Grab a piece of 2x4 at least 6" long from the rack labeled "lumber" out by the elevator (if you can’t find it, ask a PI).
3. Find an instruction sheet for the fish. It should be behind the project binder, next to the sandpaper. (Again, feel free to ask a PI)
4. Build the fish!
5. Make sure to put away all your tools when you are done.
6. Show your new fish to a front desk PI to get a stamp!
